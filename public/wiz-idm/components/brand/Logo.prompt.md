Wiz brand lockup — use in nav bars, headers, login and splash surfaces.

```jsx
<Logo />                        {/* mark + Wiz | iDM */}
<Logo variant="mark" height={28} />
<Logo suffix="EDC" height={24} />
```

Variants: `full` (default) shows the gradient glyph, the "Wiz" wordmark, a hairline divider and the product `suffix`; `mark` shows only the gradient glyph. The glyph always carries the brand gradient — never recolor it. Place on white or light surfaces; for dark/hero surfaces, set the wordmark area on a contrasting panel.
